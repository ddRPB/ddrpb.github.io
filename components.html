Components

The complex nature of radiotherapy research data requires from RPB to deal with data management within different scopes/sub-domains and integrate with several clinical or research IT systems.

Work Packages Diagram

WP0 - link to clinical systems

  • HL7 listener - NextGen Connect

WP1 - patient privacy

  • Patient identity management and pseudonym generation Mainzelliste

WP2 - clinical research

  • EDC (new) - LibreClinica
  • EDC (legacy) - OpenClinica
  • XForms render engine - Enketo
  • RPB core, portal, mobile - RPB software components

WP3 - medical imaging

  • Research PACS - Conquest
  • RPB client - de-identification and upload of DICOM data
  • DICOM viewer - Weasis
  • CTP - Clinical Trials Processor

WP4 - microscopy imaging

  • DICOM Whole Slide Imaging (WSI) - Supplement 145

WP5 - biospecimen data

  • BIO - CentraXX (commercial propriety product)

WP6 - laboratory assays data

  • LAB - LabKey

WP7 - exploration & reporting

  • LAB - study data reload and cross component data aggregation, search and reporting

WP8 - platform deployment

  • Highly depends on trials unit existing infrastructure. Recommended is normally virtual machine based deployment and dedicated reverse proxy in DMZ.
  • PM - Redmine for project management

WP9 - link to national and international research platforms

  • DKTK CCP-IT - project specific pseudonymisation
  • DKTK JIP - DICOM data export

WP10 - learning database

  • LAB - scripting for machine learning purposes
